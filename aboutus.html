ABOUT US


Laner Street Medical Centre is the peak organisation for diseases problem research, advocacy and awareness in Australia, with a time-critical mission to increase five-year survival from the current 20% to 50% by 2023. With a strong patient focus we forge global collaborations, fund innovative research and are bringing world-class clinical trials to Australia to give children and adults with diseases access to new treatments faster.


Laner Street Medical Centre opened in 1990 as a joint commitment between the Sisters of Charity and a number of specialists. The Laner Street Medical Centre is now a Centre of Excellence, providing a comprehensive range of patient services. More than 100 doctors and allied health professionals provide care for patients at the Hospital.

Our primary aim is to provide excellence in patient care that includes respect for personal dignity. Our services reflect the values of Laner Street Medical Centre Australia: Compassion, Justice, Integrity and Excellence.

The Hospital is one of 34 facilities of Laner Street Medical Centre Australia and we follow the Sisters of Charity heritage of Christ's healing mission by providing the highest standards of patient care. The emphasis on excellence in medical care is strengthened by our commitment to research and education.

We are located on the Sydney Medical Campus in Victoria Street,Sydney. The Hospital provides diagnostic and treatment services to the hospitals, including SYdney NeuroSurgery Unit. The hospital also includes Sisters of Charity Outreach and the Sydney Hospital Foundation.

Laner Street Medical Centre Alumni Association is open to all former staff members of any of the organisations of the Sydney Neurology Campus: Sydney Dental Hospital, Sydney Psychology Clinic, Sacred Brain Health Service and all associated services.

The Alumni enables people to stay in touch, directly or indirectly, with former colleagues and friends. Members are kept informed of developments on Sydney Medical Campus and news of the facilities.
